Foundation And Establishment

The vision of establishing a spiritual and educational hub in Cherwan was brought to life by a group of devoted individuals committed to faith and community service. Under the leadership of Khursheed Ahmad Mir, son of Ali Mohammad Mir and then President of Jamiat Ahl-e-Hadees Unit Cherwan, the foundation stone (Sang-e-Buniyad) of the masjid was laid.

The initial development of the masjid was overseen by Manzoor Ahmad Magaray, who served as Chairman during this pivotal phase. His dedication played a crucial role in transforming the vision into reality.

Affiliation

Jamia Ahl-e-Hadees Mohammadia Masjid Sharif is proudly affiliated with Jamiat Ahl-e-Hadees Jammu & Kashmir, Barbarshah. The masjid is managed by Jamiat Ahl-e-Hadees Unit Cherwan, which oversees its religious, educational, and welfare initiatives. This affiliation ensures alignment with the broader mission of promoting Islamic knowledge and community service.
